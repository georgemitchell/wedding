Our Story

  • Childhoods

    Late 70s-80s

    George is the baby of the family with a big gap and then two older siblings, one brother and one sister. Regina is the baby of the family with a big gap and then two older siblings, one brother and one sister. Clearly these two were meant for each other...

  • The First Email

    16 Nov 2014

    "Before you delete this email, consider one thing..." Regina would log into Match.com to find tens if not hundreds of emails from potential suitors. George can count the number of times women reached out to him on one hand. There was so much for George to be attracted to in Regina, but he first fell in love with her writing. A week of emails back and forth set the stage for what was sure to be an epic first date.

  • The First Date

    21 Nov 2014

    "Hi Regina. I'm leaving now. I've packed emergency rations, a locator beacon, and waterproof matches just in case I run into trouble on my trek. "

     

    In what was the first of many signs that George had found his true love, he didn't think twice about making the treacherous Santa Monica -> Pasadena journey on a Friday evening to meet at one of Regina's favorite restaurants: Malbec.

     

    George doesn't remember many specifics about what was discussed (although he undoubtably exagerrated his knowledge of fine wines), but by the end of the evening he was pretty much ready to get married. Regina still had some reservations...

  • Making Sure

    Dec 2014 - Feb 2015

    George was crazy about Regina, but Regina was a little bit more reserved. George's life was a bit... complicated. Christmas 2014 brought a trip back to Michigan and some contemplation on what it would mean to seriously date a guy with kids. But George was patient and persistent. He looked for excuses to send her letters or talk to her on the phone. He even learned how to use emojis. But he ultimately won her over by writing her a song.

  • The Song

    12 Feb 2015

    Historically George found dudes who play guitar to woman to be something on the cheesy end of the spectrum, but he poured his heart into this song. What he lacked in ability, he made up for in enthusiasm. Clearly something was right, because from that point on Regina and George were inseparable.

  • In Love

    13 Feb 2015

    We spent the next year and a half learning everything about each other. Regina taught George about green smoothies, the importance of planning, and Bachelor Nation. George taught Regina about Douglas Adams, counting in binary, and the difficulty of living with 3 boys.

  • We are Engaged

    15 June 2016

    With Regina being the major snoop that she is, George had to enlist Marilyn and Tom into the subterfuge in planning the proposal. Regina was spending a couple of weeks in Old Mission for summer break, so George surprised her by flying out one weekend. Marilyn took Regina out shopping in the morning while Tom picked George up at the airport so that they would be waiting for Regina when she returned home. The proposal followed soon after and despite George's wishes that they head down to the courthouse to make it official then and there, the planning for this event began in earnest.

  • This Website

    19 Dec 2016

    It took entirely too long, and I probably just should've gone with the photographer's website or some wordpress template, but I've kind of built our entire relationship on the idea that I'm good at computers, so I wasn't about to tell Regina that building this website was going to take a long time.
