Groom

George Mitchell

"The Funny One"

When asked to describe George's personality traits, the kinder members of his family will generally bandy about terms like "charming", "beloved", and "fun to be around". But the honest ones are more likely to say "sarcastic", "difficult", and "nowhere near as funny as he thinks". George was born in London, grew up in New York, went to school in the bay area, and has lived in Los Angeles for longer than he cares to remember. He's fully cognizant of the fact that he has hit the fiancee lottery.

"The Hot One"

Regina is everything that is good and pure and wonderful in the world. She somehow manages to fit right into Los Angeles despite her Michigan roots and general eschewing of the sun (too many wrinkles!) She is the best thing that's ever happened to George.
