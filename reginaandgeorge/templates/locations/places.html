Our Favorite Places

Regina adores the Traverse City area, and she has made it her mission to have George love it, too! Her grandmother, along with a group of other Chicago-based families, would spend summers in Old Mission starting in 1910! “The Strip,” a two-mile stretch of land on the West Bay with Regina’s parents’ house at the South, is dotted with old cabins and cottages, most of which are still owned by extended family. Needless to say, it is a very special place, and George proposed to Regina there last summer. Smart man...how could she say no in her favorite place in the world?!
