Old Mission

This 22-mile-long finger of land that divides East and West Grand Traverse Bays boasts a beachy coastline, rolling hillsides, cherry orchards, breathtaking views, cute fruit stands, one brewery/distillery, and 10 wineries with tasting rooms and vineyards. It is dreamy and the perfect place to spend an afternoon.
